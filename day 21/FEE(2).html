Equity Trading

Equity trading or stock trading is the buying and selling of equities in the market through your registered trading account

Bombay Stock Exchange

The Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) is the first and largest securities market in India and was established in 1875 as the Native Share and Stock Brokers' Association.

National Stock Exchange

The National Stock Exchange of India, or NSE, is one of the country's leading financial markets, offering a unified trading interface for stockholders.

Systematic Investment Plan

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is an investment tool which allows the investor to invest a fixed amount at regular intervals in a Mutual Fund scheme. SIP works by investing a fixed amount at a defined frequency

Exchange-Traded Fund

An exchange-traded fund (ETF) is a type of pooled investment security that operates much like a mutual fund.
